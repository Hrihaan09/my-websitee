Codections

Project: Codections

Date: May 2024

Codections is an interactive game that I developed as a part of my computer science coursework. The objective of the game is to solve coding challenges within a limited time frame, earning points for correct solutions and progressing through levels. I was responsible for designing the game mechanics, implementing the coding challenges, and testing the game for functionality and user experience. Through this project, I gained a deeper understanding of programming concepts and honed my problem-solving skills.

Emergency Calculator

Course: Emergency Calculator

Date: December 2023

For this project, we developed an Emergency Calculator App designed to aid our classroom during natural disasters. The app allowed users to post messages to other students and teachers, requesting or offering help in emergency situations. My role in the project involved frontend development and UI design. I learned about user interface design principles and the importance of clear communication during emergencies. Challenges included integrating real-time messaging functionality and ensuring user privacy and security. This project highlighted my teamwork skills as we collaborated closely to meet tight deadlines and ensure the app's effectiveness in emergency scenarios.
